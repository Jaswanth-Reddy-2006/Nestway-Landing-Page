About Nestway

Nestway is a revolutionary travel app designed to transform how guests discover and book accommodations across India. Our platform leverages cutting-edge AI to provide deeply personalized tour recommendations, offering comprehensive trip planning and real-time tracking for a seamless travel experience.

Key Features

  • 🎙️ AI Voice-Based Planning: Travelers can interact with the system through voice commands, making planning more natural and personalized.
  • 🌍 360° Destination Previews: Users get immersive previews of travel spots before booking, offering a virtual experience of the destination.
  • 💰 Budget-Based Suggestions: TripWhiz recommends destinations and packages tailored to the user’s financial limits.
  • 🧑‍✈️ Tour Guide Included: The platform ensures users have access to guided experiences, adding convenience and depth to the trip.
  • 🚫 No Third-Party Redirection: All services are provided within the TripWhiz ecosystem—no bouncing to other sites.
  • 💳 One-Time Payment: Simplified billing: users pay once without dealing with recurring fees or hidden costs.
